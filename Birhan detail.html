Introducing Berhan: The Inclusive Online dynamic Study And Examination Tool Berhan is an app designed for study assistance and online exams, catering to visually impaired students. It offers advanced accessibility features such as text-to-speech and voice recognition for visually impaired students. Regular students benefit from a user-friendly interface and customizable settings. With Berhan, educators can easily administer secure exams and monitor progress. Experience inclusivity and excellence with Berhan, the future of online exams.
